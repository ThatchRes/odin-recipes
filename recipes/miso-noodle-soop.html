Miso Noodle Soup

Description

This is a quick Miso Noodle Soup recipe taking only 15 minutes. This dish combines raman noodles, miso paste, and seasoning to create a comforting home-cooked dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp finely grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tsp finely grated garlic
  • 1/3 cup thinly sliced leek
  • 1 1/2 cups thinly sliced cabbage
  • 1/2 cup enoki mushrooms
  • 4 ounces refrigerated fresh ramen noodles
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp black sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp sliced green onion

Steps

  1. Step 1: Crack eggs into a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Cook until outer edges become opaque, about 1 minute. Turn once; cook until whites are completely set, about 3 minutes; set eggs aside.
  2. Step 2: Heat sesame oil in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir ginger, garlic, leek, cabbage, and mushrooms until fragrant, about 1 minute. Stir in miso paste; gradually whisk in water and bring to a boil. Add in ramen noodles; reduce heat and simmer until noodles soften, about 3 minutes. Season with soy sauce. Divide into two bowls; top each serving with egg. Garnish with sesame seeds and scallions.
